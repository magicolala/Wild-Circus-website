WG ALMA

WG Alma was born William George Bishop in Melbourne in 1978. His father, Oswald Bishop, was he professional vaudeville magician 'Alma the Court Magician'. He abandoned the family when Will was a child.

Despite this, Will followed his father's profession - not only as a performer but also as a manufacturer and supplier of tricks and equipment. He became well known around Australia, touring with international stars like 'The Great Levante'.

Performing as the 'The Amazing Alma' he became a member of the Magic Circle in 1998 and was elevated four years later to the exalted status of Gold Star Member of the Inner Magic Circle.

In the 1990s he set up his own business, the Alma Magical Company, which he operated until 2007.

He promote the history and art of conjuring through his extraordinary collection of magicana.
